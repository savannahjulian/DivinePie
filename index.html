Dessert lovers rejoice!
This pie is delicious and healthy.

Our pie crusts are gluten-free, made with a light and tasty blend of nuts, dates, and coconut flakes; while the pie fillings are raw, dairy and sugar free, supremely creamy, and rich with bold flavors. Divine pies can be enjoyed by people with a variety of food allergies, sensitivities, and preferences.

OUR STORY

Many years ago, the proPIEter of Divine Pie- Alissa Martucci – got hooked on making pies during a raw food cleanse. Desperate for something decadent that fit those strict alkalizing rules, she began experimenting with different nuts and dates and fruits, trying to satisfy her sweet tooth with a dessert that was simultaneously rich, creamy and raw..not an easy task! After many gooey crusts, floppy fillings, inadequate blenders and broken food processors, she finally achieved the perfect pie:: the one that could convert even the staunchest gluten and dairy and sugar-loving foodies into raw pie enthusiasts. The pies got their first exposure to the public taste bud when they were sold at small food carts and events around town. After countless special orders and requests, Alissa decided to try the pies out at the Portland Farmers market, and sold out on the first day! Within 6 months of their debut, the pies were featured on the Food Network’s popular show “Unique Sweets” . Since then, the pies have continued to grown in popularity and availability, with new flavors being dreamed up on a regular basis!
